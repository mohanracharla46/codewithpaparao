Frequently Asked Questions

Clear details about curriculum structures, certification access, and batch types.

Can absolute beginners join these courses?
Yes! Our C Programming and Python Core tracks are designed from absolute scratch. We don't assume prior development experience, teaching you both logic and execution from day one.
Are the certificates verified?
Yes, every course completion certificate contains a unique alphanumeric validation code. This can be shared on LinkedIn, and employers can verify authenticity directly on our platform.
How do I get help if I get stuck on a coding assignment?
Every active course comes with access to our student dashboard bookmark system and community channels, where mentors and peers answer query logs daily.
Are the batches live or recorded?
We offer a hybrid approach. The self-paced modules are recorded in high quality, while live cohorts feature weekly interactive schedules, lab assessments, and live QA review blocks.
